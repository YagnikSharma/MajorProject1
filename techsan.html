Track Your Mind and Health with NeuroBand

NeuroBand uses advanced EEG technology in a headband and sensors in a smartwatch to monitor your brainwaves, pulse rate, and daily activity. With AI-powered insights, the app helps you stay in tune with your mental and physical well-being, providing real-time feedback and health reports.

EEG Headband

Track brainwaves and control IoT devices with mental focus. Detect stress and mental health insights.

Smartwatch Sensors

IMU and PPG sensors monitor real-time pulse rate and activity patterns to help you stay healthy.

AI-Powered Insights

Get personalized weekly health reports and emergency alerts. Let AI guide your well-being decisions.
